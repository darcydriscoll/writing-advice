Example Structure: Abyss and Rebirth

❗ Motivation and Definition

If your characters are going through emotional ups and downs over the course of your story, there is necessarily going to be a 'lowest point' out of all of these. This structure formalises this lowest point with The Abyss, and gives it a special function in collaboration with The Rebirth: all characters who enter the lowest point must make their most important choice (or sacrifice) to come out of it still pursuing their desired change. You might also think of this as the climax of the story, the highest point of tension on which the fate of the characters and/or their world is riding.

❓ Caveats

The nature of The Rebirth is malleable. Maybe your characters undergo Rebirth and decide to give up on the change they originally intended to project.
