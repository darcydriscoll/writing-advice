Good Writing Advice is a Tool

üìî How to Use Writing Advice

It can be tempting to take writing advice at face value, especially when you're starting out or struggling. Most advice comes from cults of personality, which strengthens this temptation. But before you absorb some pieces of advice, you might find it useful to consider why you write in the first place -- what writing means to you.

Every piece of advice comes from someone with a different definition of what writing means to them. They are trying to project that meaning through the advice they give. Some meanings might resonate with you. Some might not. Still others might damage your self-worth, or weaken your confidence. Put advice in context, use it as a tool, and it will serve you better.
